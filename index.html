The Town of Princeville

Princeville, North Carolina, located south east of Rocky Mount, is a small town recognized as the first independently governed African American community chartered in the United States. Princeville remains around 92% African American to this day and is an important cultural hub for the area.

Our Team

▼

Our team consists of dedicated researchers, historians, and community members working together to preserve and document Princeville's rich history.

Our Projects

▼

We are engaged in various projects to document and preserve Princeville's history, including the Cemetery Database, Community Garden, and Historical Maps.

Our Impacts

▼

Through our work, we've helped preserve important historical records, engage the community, and ensure Princeville's legacy for future generations.
